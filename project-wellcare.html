WellCare

Description

WellCare diagnostics center wishes to be pioneers in the online health care sector of Bangladesh. The Covid insurgence lifted the stigma of remote health care, but its system is still immature. WellCare will provide online medical services & a centralized platform for medical documents.

What issues are we solving?

The immaturity of the online health care structure in Bangladesh is frustrating for the people.

Finding Doctors & booking an appointment is a tedious and exhausting process.

People with health concerns that impact mobility can’t readily receive treatment from home.

Patients are notorious for mismanaging medical records, which affects the diagnosis process.

Our Goals

Provide the people of Bangladesh with proper online healthcare services through a mobile app.

  • Consult with doctors from home, at a time of one's choice.
  • Check the market price of medicines & get medicine delivered from the Wellcare Pharmacy.
  • Book diagnostic tests and receive digital results.
  • Pay conveniently through bkash & nagad.
  • Chat instantly to avail medical services.

Create a centralized platform for patients medical records, trace prescriptions, diagnosis reports & test results.

Increase cost-efficiency by cutting down on labor, time and space resources.

Researching our Audience

A survey conducted by WellCare and a few interviews conducted by me revealed key data for decision making, and solidified the audience we will cater to.

Key Findings

  • Most people own a phone but not a pc.
  • Commonly used apps support Bangla language options.
  • People aged above 40 don’t book their own appointments, their younger or more educated family members do.
  • The emergency tele-medical services provided during Covid were greatly appreciated.
  • People worry over getting scammed with medicine costs.

Defining the Audience

Patients

  • People seeking medical advice & treatment.
  • People with pre-existing medical conditions & diagnosis history.

Relatives or Carer of patients

  • People who support patients due to various reasons.
